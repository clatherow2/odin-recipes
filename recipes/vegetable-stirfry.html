Vegetable Stir-Fry

Home

Ingredients

  • Broccoli florets
  • Red and yellow bell peppers (sliced)
  • Carrots (julienned)
  • Snap peas
  • Mushrooms (sliced)
  • Garlic (minced)
  • Soy sauce
  • Sesame oil
  • Cornstarch (optional, for thickening)
  • Sesame seeds (for garnish)
  • Cooked rice (for serving)

Steps

  1. Heat sesame oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add garlic and sauté for 30 seconds until fragrant.
  3. Add broccoli, carrots, and snap peas; stir-fry for 3–4 minutes.
  4. Add bell peppers and mushrooms; continue stir-frying for another 2–3 minutes.
  5. Pour in soy sauce and stir to coat vegetables evenly.
  6. If desired, mix cornstarch with a bit of water and add to thicken the sauce.
  7. Cook for another minute until sauce is glossy and vegetables are tender-crisp.
  8. Serve hot over steamed rice and garnish with sesame seeds.
