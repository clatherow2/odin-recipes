Chicken Alfredo Pasta

Home

Ingredients

  • Fettuccine pasta
  • Boneless chicken breasts
  • Butter
  • Heavy cream
  • Garlic (minced)
  • Parmesan cheese (grated)
  • Salt and pepper
  • Fresh parsley (chopped)

Steps

  1. Cook fettuccine pasta according to package instructions; drain and set aside.
  2. Season chicken breasts with salt and pepper, then grill or pan-sear until fully cooked; slice into strips.
  3. In a saucepan, melt butter and saut√© garlic until fragrant.
  4. Add heavy cream and bring to a gentle simmer.
  5. Stir in grated Parmesan cheese until the sauce thickens.
  6. Add cooked pasta to the sauce and toss to coat evenly.
  7. Top with sliced chicken and garnish with chopped parsley.
  8. Serve hot with extra Parmesan on the side if desired.
