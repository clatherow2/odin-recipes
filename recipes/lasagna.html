Lasagna

Home

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground meat (beef or pork)
  • Tomato-based meat sauce
  • Ricotta or cottage cheese
  • Mozzarella and Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Chopped parsley
  • Cherry tomatoes (whole and halved)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions; drain and set aside.
  3. Prepare the meat sauce by browning ground meat and adding tomato sauce and seasonings.
  4. Mix ricotta or cottage cheese with chopped parsley and a bit of salt.
  5. In a baking dish, layer noodles, meat sauce, cheese mixture, and shredded mozzarella.
  6. Repeat layers until ingredients are used up, finishing with a generous layer of cheese on top.
  7. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes, then uncover and bake for another 15 minutes until bubbly and golden.
  8. Let the lasagna rest for 10 minutes before serving.
  9. Garnish with fresh basil and serve with cherry tomatoes on the side.
